Since 2013, the Citi Bike system, a bicycle sharing project in New York City, has experienced rapid and sustained growth.
Studies showed that from 2014 to 2017, the use of bike-sharing systems in NYC saved 13,370t of oil equivalent and decreased 30,070t of CO2 emission.
